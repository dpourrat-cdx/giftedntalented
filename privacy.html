Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

We only save your explorer name and mission scores. Your parent can delete them anytime. We don't track you or sell your information.

1. Who we are

Captain Nova's Rocket Mission is a free, story-driven educational app for children.

Operator: [operator name] — [contact email address]

This section must be filled in before this policy is published. Under GDPR (Art. 13), COPPA (16 CFR 312.4), and similar laws, the operator's real name and a working contact email address are required.

2. What data we collect

We collect only what is needed to run the app:

  • Explorer name — a first name or nickname chosen by the child.
  • Mission scores and percentages — the child's best mission score.
  • Mission attempt history — questions selected, answers given, and timing data.
  • Device-local cache — scores saved in your browser's localStorage for faster load and offline display.
  • Optional push notification token — a device identifier for push notifications, only if the feature is enabled.

We do not collect:

  • Email addresses, full names, home addresses, or phone numbers
  • Cookies
  • Location data
  • Browsing history or cross-site tracking identifiers
  • Any analytics or third-party tracking data

3. Why we collect it

  • To save and display mission scores between sessions
  • To enable the parent reset and per-explorer deletion features
  • To provide the educational gameplay experience

4. How we store it

Data Where stored Provider
Explorer names, scores, attempts Online database Supabase Inc. (us-west-2, Oregon, USA)
Backend server (API) Cloud hosting Render (render.com)
Optional push notifications Cloud messaging Google Firebase Cloud Messaging
Score cache This device only (browser localStorage) First-party — stays on your device

The app is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub serves the static app files only and does not receive your child's personal data.

5. How long we keep it

Data type Kept for
Best scores 12 months after last activity, then automatically deleted
Mission attempt history 30 days after completion, then automatically deleted
Device cache (localStorage) Until cleared by the parent, or after 30 days of no activity
Push notification token 90 days of inactivity, then automatically deleted

6. Who we share it with

We do not sell data. We do not share data with advertisers or marketers.

Data is shared only with the service providers listed in Section 4, solely to operate the app:

  • Supabase Inc. — database hosting
  • Render — backend server hosting
  • Google LLC / Firebase — optional push notifications only

We do not share data with any other third parties.

7. International data transfers

Our database and backend server are hosted in the United States (Oregon, us-west-2). If you are in the EU, UK, or another jurisdiction outside the US, your child's data is transferred to the US to operate the app.

We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) with Supabase Inc. as the legal mechanism for these transfers, as required under GDPR Art. 46.

8. Your rights as a parent

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your child's data. The app's Parent Controls panel lets you:

  • View your child's data — download a copy of all saved scores and attempts.
  • Delete one explorer — permanently remove one child's records without affecting others.
  • Delete all records — permanently remove all saved data.

To exercise rights not covered by the in-app controls (such as a formal access request or a complaint), contact us at: [contact email address]

You may also file a complaint with your local data protection authority:

  • EU: Your national supervisory authority (see edpb.europa.eu)
  • UK: Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk)
  • US: Federal Trade Commission (ftc.gov/coppa)

9. Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).
  • Data at rest is encrypted by Supabase.
  • The admin PIN is stored as a bcrypt hash — we cannot see it.
  • Sensitive admin actions are rate-limited.
  • Content Security Policy and frame-busting protections are in place.

10. Changes to this policy

We will update this page when the policy changes. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
